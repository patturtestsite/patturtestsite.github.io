Home of Hope: Youth Chapter

Mission Statement

Home of Hope's Youth Chapter aims to provide educational opportunities to disadvantaged and underprivileged individuals to support them in becoming successful young adults.

What is Home of Hope?

Home of Hope is a nonprofit organization (501c3) that supports underprivileged youths by granting them access to basic educational amenities such as computer skills, linguistic fluency, and mental health resources. To learn more about our youth officer team, click below.
