Events

Each youth chapter hosts independently run events ranging from sales promotions to door-to-door fundraising. Take a look at some of the major events the chapters have organized.

HOH Gala

The Youth Chapters assist HOH Inc in leading the annual HOH Gala, where representatives from each youth chapter collect funds from donors. Distinguished members of each Youth Chapter are also recognized tonight, making the night all the more magical. At the 2019 Gala, there were 350 attendees and $180,000 was raised.

Homeless Drive

We partnered with other student-run organizations such as CSF to collect supplies for homeless shelters around the Bay Area. Over 600 items were collected, ranging from clothes to hygiene products used during the COVID-19 Pandemic.

Food Drive and Holi Festival

Unfortunately, due to the COVID-19 Pandemic, the Youth Chapters' annual Holi Cultural festival and their Food Drive were canceled. However, we look forward to hold these events in 2021 if possible.
