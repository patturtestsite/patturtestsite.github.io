What Makes Us Different?

Welcome to STAC Programming, a programming school targeted towards kids of around grades 5 - 8. Our goal is to introduce our students to programming and give them the skills, experience, and passion they need in order to pursue their path in programming. Why us? Often in large classes, students are timid or just not willing enough to ask questions in class or participate. At STAC Programming, we encourage student-teacher relations and all of our teachers are young qualified high school students, resulting in increased learning as students are more comfortable to ask questions and participate in class.
