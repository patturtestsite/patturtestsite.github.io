INSPIRE SCIENCE IN YOUTH.

ABOUT US

Hello and welcome to Inspire Science in Youth! We are a team of two science lovers hoping to elicit a passion for STEM in young minds. ISY summer sessions are comprised of mini lessons discussing various disciplines of science including, but not limited to, chemistry, biology, and physics. Accompanying these lessons are labs and activities concerning the principles explored during the lessons to provide fun hands on learning.
