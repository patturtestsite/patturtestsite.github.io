WHY IS SCIENCE IMPORTANT?

In a world filled with the products of scientific inquiry, scientific literacy has become a necessity for everyone. Everyone needs to use scientific information to make choices that arise every day. From understanding gravity to the spherical shape of the Earth to climate change, science helps us understand and interact with the world around us. As we move from understanding the basic laws of physics, to realizing that even the basic laws are far more complex than we had ever imagined, we gain more insight in how we can better solve problems in our world. That being so, we believe that the value of science in early childhood education is of imperative nature. To harness their proclivity for curiosity, young children should learn science through first-hand investigative experiences and active involvement. They should ask questions, seek answers, collect data and conduct investigations. Our mission here at Hicksville ISY is to provide young children just that - an early STEM experience that not only ensures a strong academic foundation, but also fosters the excitement and personal fulfillment that can come from understanding and learning about the world of science.
